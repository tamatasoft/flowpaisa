Our Mission

Flowpaisa is a comprehensive personal finance and cash flow manager. Our mission is to empower users to track their expenses, incomes, receivables, and payables all in one place. With features like receipt scanning, WhatsApp integration, and beautiful analytics, we make financial management professional yet simple.

Advanced Capabilities

Flowpaisa goes beyond basic tracking with features designed for real-world scenarios:

Linked Transactions

Automatically connect expenses to receivables. Paid for a friend's lunch? Record the expense and link it to a contact to instantly create a receivable record.

Smart Split Bill

Easily divide group expenses. Split bills equally or by percentage among multiple contacts, and let Flowpaisa handle the individual debt tracking.

Deep Linking

Share transaction details via unique deep links. Recipients can import shared transactions directly into their own Flowpaisa app with a single tap.

Multilingual Support

Flowpaisa speaks your language. Full support for English, Hindi, and Urdu to ensure a comfortable experience for everyone.

Privacy Policy

We value your privacy above all else. Your financial data is sensitive, and we believe it should remain yours.

Local Storage: Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not have central servers that store your financial records.

Private Cloud: Optional cloud sync and backups are performed using your own Google Drive account. This ensures that even in the cloud, your data is within your private ecosystem.

No Data Selling: We do not track your spending habits for advertising, nor do we sell your data to third parties.

Developed By

Flowpaisa is proudly developed by TamataSoft. We are committed to building high-quality, professional tools that respect user privacy and provide exceptional value.
