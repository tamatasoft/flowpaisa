Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 02, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Flowpaisa ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe experience while managing your personal finances.

1. Information Collection and Use

Contacts Data (android.permission.READ_CONTACTS)

Flowpaisa allows you to link financial transactions (Incomes, Expenses, Payables, and Receivables) to your existing phone contacts for easier management.

  • Local Access Only: The App reads your contact list locally on your device to allow you to select a name and phone number for a transaction.
  • No Uploading: We DO NOT upload your contacts to our servers, nor do we share them with any third parties. Your contact data stays strictly on your device.

Financial Data

All transaction data, categories, and cashbook entries you create are stored locally on your device using a secure database.

  • Backup: If you choose to use the "Cloud Backup" feature, your data is encrypted and uploaded to your own Google Drive account. We do not have access to this data.

Camera and Images

The App requests access to your camera and gallery only when you choose to scan a receipt or attach an image to a transaction. This data is processed locally for OCR (Text Recognition) and stored on your device.

2. Permissions We Request

  • Read Contacts: To link transactions to people in your phone book.
  • Camera/Storage: To scan and save receipts.
  • Internet: To process In-App Purchases and perform Cloud Backups to your Google Drive.
  • Billing: To manage your Premium subscription via Google Play.

3. Data Safety & Security

Flowpaisa is designed with a "Local First" philosophy. We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties. Since data is stored locally, it is deleted if you uninstall the App (unless you have a manual backup on Google Drive).

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: For App functionality and secure In-App Purchases.
  • Firebase Analytics: To help us understand app performance and fix crashes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
